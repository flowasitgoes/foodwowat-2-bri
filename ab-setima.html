Title

AB Setima

Category

Type Design

Year

2024

AB Setima is a sans-serif display typeface that merges Art Deco
geometric foundations with contemporary sensibilities. Its
proportions echo historical precedents while maintaining a distinct
visual character. At larger sizes, AB Setima reveals its crafted details
— tapered inner angles reminiscent of traditional printing techniques.
The typeface includes variants and discretionary ligatures for
compositional flexibility.

AB Setima is suited for environments balancing refinement and
approachability: restaurant identities, hotel graphics, and event
communications. It delivers distinction without excess.

Its proportions echo historical precedents while maintaining a distinct visual character. At larger sizes, AB Setima reveals its crafted details — tapered inner angles reminiscent of traditional printing techniques. The typeface includes variants and discretionary ligatures for compositional flexibility.

AB Setima is suited for environments balancing refinement and approachability: restaurant identities, hotel graphics, and event communications. It delivers distinction without excess.
