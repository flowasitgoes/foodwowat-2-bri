Title

10 thoughts on making a personal portfolio

Date

2025-05-08

Topic

Thoughts

Body

In no particular order:

  1. It’s only as important as you think it is.

  2. Better designers have managed with less.

  3. Sometimes the portfolio is part of the Work in a sort of “the medium is the message” kind of way but that’s not always the case.

  4. All work should be handled as archival material. Treat it with respect and maybe others will too.

  5. Next time, let another designer design it for you.

  6. A good dev is like a good barber.

  7. It is never complete. It will never be perfect. It is the abyss you shouldn’t look too deep into, either way.

  8. Think in seasons. Let shit compost.

  9. It should be fun but if it isn’t a bit painful maybe it’s not worth it.

  10. If you don’t have time to update it that might be a good sign.

  11. [Extra] Once done, move on.
